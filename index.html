Visit

León is a city of Spain, capital of the province of León and part of the autonomous community of Castile and León, in the northwest of the Iberian Peninsula. León may not be a very well-known Spanish city, but it does offer visitors a fantastic variety of things to see and do. León is home to a wealth of grand, historic buildings, which include some of the most important in Spain.

You cannot leave León without viiting:

Santa Maria de León Cathedral

Gothic jewel from the 13th century and one of the emblems of the city, along with its stained glass windows, considered among the best in the world

St Isidore's Basilica

One of the most complete pieces of the Romanesque. A visit to the Royal Pantheon is essential, with the so-called "Sistine Chapel of Romanesque Art"

MUSAC museum

The MUSAC is one of the architecctural jewels of León, as interesting on the outside as on the inside. An example of the contemporary art scene

San Marcos

Architectural landmark of the first order in the Jacobean route, its construction was born from the donation made by the Infanta Dona Sancha in the 12th century.

The Botines Building
