Visit

León is a city in Spain, capital of the province of León and part of the autonomous community of Castile and León, in the northwest of the Iberian Peninsula. León may not be a very well-known Spanish city, but it does offer visitors a fantastic variety of things to see and do. León is home to a wealth of grand, historical buildings, which include some of the most important in Spain.

You cannot leave León without visiting:

Santa Maria de León Cathedral

A gothic jewel from the 13th century along with its stained glass windows, which are World Heritaged. It is considered among the best in the world, and the most visited monument in León. Visit Cathedral

St. Isidore's Basilica

One of the most complete pieces of Romanesque architecture. In the museum you must visit the Royal Pantheon, which is often refered to as the "Sistine Chapel of Romanesque Art". Visit St Isidoro

MUSAC museum

The MUSAC is one of the iconic buildings in León. Not only an example of the contemporary art scene, but also an architectural gem. Visit MUSAC

San Marcos

The longest and one of the best Renaissance façade in Spain. Nowadays, it accommodates the hotel Parador of León. Visit San Marcos

The Botines Building

Antonio Gaudi, a famous architect who has built numerous buildings including the famous Barcelona cathedral, constructed this magnificent building in the centre of León at the end of the 19th century. Visit Casa Botines
