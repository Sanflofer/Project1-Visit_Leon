Eat

León is well known for its tapas, not only due to their quality and variety, but also because they are free! We will explore some of the most famous bars in the most popular neighbourhoods: Barrio Húmedo and Barrio Romántico.

Barrio Húmedo

The Barrio Húmedo of León is one of the best known areas in the city.

It occupies part of the old town and is located to the right of the popular Calle Ancha Street, which is famous for being full of tapas bars. It is also adjacent to very popular tourist attractions, such as the Cathedral.

Some of the best tapas on offer in that district are:

Croqueta from El Rebote bar

Address: Plaza San Martín, 9

Pizza from La Competencia bar

Address: Calle Matasiete, 9

Garlic soup from El Gaucho bar

Address: Calle Azabachería, 6

Chorizo from Taberna Oriente Medio bar

Address: Calle Juan de Arfe, 8

Calamari from Latino Bar

Address: Plaza San Martín, 10

Cold meat from Jamon Jamon bar

Address: Calle Cardiles, 1

Barrio Romántico

Originally known as the Barrio del Cid, the Romantic Quarter is located to the left of Calle Ancha. Like the Barrio Húmedo, it adjoins various tourist attractions such as the León Museum and Casa Botines, which occupies another part of the old town.

Some of the best tapas on offer in that district are:

Bocadillo de pringa from El Gallinero

Address: Plaza Torres de Omaña, 6

Torrija salada from Gastrobar Cordero bar

Address: Calle Recoletas, 1

Salmorejo from Camarote Madrid bar

Address: Calle Cervantes, 8

Picadillo with potatoes from La Trebede

Address: Plaza Torres de Omaña, 1

Rice with vegetables from El Colibrin

Address: Calle de Fernando G. Regueral, 2

Potatoes with sauce from La Ribera

Address: Calle de Fernando G. Regueral, 8

In León it is very common to order a "corto" as a drink, which is just a smaller beer, about 20cl.

You can easily have lunch/dinner with just tapas. As a rule of thumb, 5-7 tapas are more than enough!

A piece of advice, please be careful how much you drink, otherwise you may end up being a little tipsy.

The Barrio Húmedo and the Barrio Romántico are the two most famous neighbourhoods, nonetheless, Picara Square and Junta De Castilla Y Leon districts are very enjoyable too.
